Dalgona Coffee

Dalgona Coffee is a South Korean fantasy recipe!!!This dish went viral on WhatsApp Status, across the world. It was highly treated as Lockdown Activity, nominating one of the friends to try it. It is pretty much easy to make.

About Chef

Chef. Vaidehi is the youngest chef. Though she did not try out the recipe personally, she has helped us in understanding what the recipe is!!!

Ingredients

  • Hot Water
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Coffee Powder
Step 1: Add Hot Water and Coffee powder and whisk
Add a cup of Hot Water into a bowl, add 2 tablespoons of coffee powder and 3 tablespoons of sugar to it.

Step 2: Whisk, Whisk, Whisk until you get a fluffy mixture
Use a hand held mixer to whisk the coffee powder, sugar and hot water mixture, till it turns to a flufffy creamy mixture.
Step3: Serve it with hot milk
Take a cup of hot /cold milk, and add the coffee cream on the top. Serve it with a spoon and a straw. Enjoy your Lockdown recipe!!!
